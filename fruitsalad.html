Fruit Salad @ the Rutledge House

Ingredients

  1. 2 tablespoons honey
  2. 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice (from about 1 lime)
  3. 1 pint fresh strawberries, hulled and cut in half
  4. 1/2 pint fresh blueberries
  5. 1 large peach, pitted and cut into 1-inch dice
  6. 1 cup seedless green grapes (about 6 ounces), halved
  7. 1 tablespoon coarsely chopped fresh mint leaves

Recipe

  1. Whisk the honey and lime juice together in a large bowl until combined. Add the strawberries, blueberries, peach, and grapes. Toss gently until evenly mixed. Sprinkle with the mint and serve.
